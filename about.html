Ben Eisenberger is an Omaha, Nebraska-based guitarist and songwriter, and a graduate of the University of Nebraska at Omahaâ€™s classical guitar program. His debut solo release Three Islands, a collection of warm and pensive acoustic songs, was released on April 24th, 2018 on cassette and digitally. A nationwide solo tour followed in September of 2018, and a midwest and southern tour in March 2019.
In 2015, Ben co-founded the experimental collective Screaming Plastic, which has released two cassettes of live improvisations and whose debut album was funded by a New Music USA grant. He also plays guitar in the band Hussies, who have been active in the Omaha music scene since 2012, and have a forthcoming cassette to be released in early 2019.
In addition to original music, Ben also does contracted work for musicals, choirs, and weddings, and also teaches private lessons.
